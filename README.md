# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales Data Analysis & Cleaning Project

> Transforming raw E-Commerce sales data into meaningful business insights using Python, Pandas, and Matplotlib.

## 📖 Project Overview

This project demonstrates the complete Data Cleaning and Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) process on an E-Commerce Sales dataset containing **1,200 records** and **15 features**. The main objective was to improve data quality, prepare the dataset for analysis, and extract valuable business insights from sales data.

Using Python libraries such as **Pandas**, **NumPy**, and **Matplotlib**, the dataset was cleaned by handling missing values, removing duplicate records, validating data consistency, and standardizing the data structure. After preprocessing, exploratory data analysis was performed to understand customer purchasing behavior, payment preferences, product performance, referral source effectiveness, and overall revenue trends.

The analysis generated several business insights, including a total revenue of **₹1,264,761.96**, identification of top-performing products, most preferred payment methods, and major customer acquisition channels. This project showcases practical data analytics skills and demonstrates the process of transforming raw data into actionable insights.

## 📈 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

### 💳 Payment Method Analysis

Customer payment behavior was analyzed to identify preferred transaction methods.

| Payment Method | Orders |
|---------------|--------:|
| Online | 258 |
| Cash | 246 |
| Credit Card | 234 |
| Debit Card | 232 |
| Gift Card | 230 |

**Insight:** Online Payments were the most preferred payment method among customers.

### 📦 Order Status Analysis

Orders were distributed across multiple fulfillment stages:

- Cancelled
- Returned
- Pending
- Shipped
- Delivered

**Insight:** Order statuses show a balanced distribution, helping evaluate operational performance and customer satisfaction.

### 🏆 Top Revenue-Generating Products

| Product | Revenue |
|----------|----------:|
| Chair | ₹195,620 |
| Printer | ₹195,613 |
| Laptop | ₹192,127 |
| Tablet | ₹186,569 |
| Monitor | ₹175,651 |

**Insight:** Chairs and Printers generated the highest revenue, making them the best-performing products in the dataset.

### 🌐 Referral Source Analysis

Traffic sources contributing to customer orders:

- Instagram
- Email Marketing
- Google Search
- Facebook
- Referral Links

**Insight:** Instagram and Email Marketing were among the most effective customer acquisition channels.

### 💰 Revenue Analysis

- **Total Revenue Generated:** ₹1,264,761.96
- Revenue was analyzed across different payment methods and product categories to identify major contributors to sales performance.

### 📊 Sales Distribution Analysis

A histogram of Total Price was used to analyze order value distribution and customer purchasing patterns.

**Insight:** Sales were distributed across multiple price ranges, providing a better understanding of customer spending behavior.
